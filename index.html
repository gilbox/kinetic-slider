Kinetic Slider for AngularJS

by Gil Birman

Features

  • Compatible with Responsive and Fluid layouts
  • Touch-friendly
  • Drag-friendly
  • Scroll-wheel-friendly
  • Mobile-friendly
  • Desktop-friendly
  • Doesn't impede vertical scrolling
  • Fake physics (could use improvement)
  • No jQuery dependencies

Requirements

  • AngularJS
  • hamster.js
  • angular-mousewheel
